================================================================================
                          SUMMARY
================================================================================

The two reports serve DIFFERENT purposes:

1. unconstrained_endpoints.rpt = Partition standalone STA
   - Shows: All ports without timing constraints
   - Useful for: Identifying interface specs needed
   - Scope: Single partition analysis

2. bu_uc.rpt = Chip-level bottom-up STA
   - Shows: Paths unconstrained after chip integration
   - Useful for: Tracking what needs waivers at chip level
   - Scope: Full chip analysis

Both are valid reports - they just answer DIFFERENT questions!
