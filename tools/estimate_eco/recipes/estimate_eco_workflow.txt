================================================================================
  RECIPE: estimate_eco Full Path Analysis Workflow
================================================================================

GOAL: Determine real ECO potential for a failing path.

STEP 1: GET THE WORST PATH
  sg soc -c "pt_client.pl -m latest_gfcn2clienta0_bu_prp_func.max_high.T_85.typical \
    -c 'report_timing -through icore0/par_meu/<signal> -max_paths 1 -pba_mode path'"

STEP 2: IDENTIFY CELLS ON THE PATH
  Look at the timing path report. Note each cell instance.
  Focus on: combinational logic cells (not FFs).

STEP 3: ESTIMATE SIZE_CELL FOR EACH CELL
  For each cell on the path:
    estimate_eco -type size_cell -sort_by slack -nosplit [get_cells <cell>]

  Record:
    Cell           Current_Ref    Best_Alternative    Delta
    <inst>         <ref_name>     <alt or *current*>  <ps>

STEP 4: ESTIMATE INSERT_BUFFER ON LONG NETS
  Look for high-delay nets in the path report.
  For each long net pin:
    estimate_eco -type insert_buffer -sort_by slack -nosplit \
      -lib_cells {BUFFD4BWP156HNPPN3P48CPDULVT BUFFD8BWP156HNPPN3P48CPDULVT} \
      [get_pins <pin>]

STEP 5: CHECK DONT_USE
  Cross-check suggested cells against illegal_cells_dont_use.txt:
    - No ELVT cells
    - No LVTLL cells
    - No PNNP OD flavor cells

STEP 6: SUM VERIFIED DELTAS
  Total ECO potential = sum of individual cell deltas.
  Be conservative: use 70% of estimated total for planning.

STEP 7: REPORT
  Use skills/reporting/templates/eco_impact.txt template.
  Include: which cells to change, estimated gain, risk.

DECISION:
  Total potential > 10ps  -> worth pursuing ECO
  Total potential 5-10ps  -> marginal, discuss with PO
  Total potential < 5ps   -> ECO not viable, try clock push/spec
================================================================================
