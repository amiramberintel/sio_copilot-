 SECTION 7: ASSUMPTIONS & RISKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Assumptions:
  1. Clock balance ECO reduces skew by ~70ps average (based on ICG proximity)
  2. 70% of LCS paths fully close after clock ECO
  3. Vt swap + upsize gives ~10ps average improvement per data path
  4. 90% of SM (small violation) paths close with any ECO
  5. No illegal cell violations (must check against illegal.txt)

  Risks:
  1. Hold degradation — Vt swap (faster cells) may worsen hold timing
     Mitigation: monitor hold after each step, insert hold buffers if needed
  2. Max transition — upsizing output may cause upstream tran violations
     Mitigation: check max_tran after upsize step
  3. DRC increase — new cells + routing may add DRCs
     Mitigation: route_eco + legalize after each step
  4. Leakage — ULVT cells consume more power
     Mitigation: only swap cells on critical paths, not blanket swap
  5. FEV — any ECO requires FEV re-verification
     Mitigation: run FEV after all ECOs complete
